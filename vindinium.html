What is Vindinium?

Vindinium

Vindinium is a Artificial Intelligence programming challenge. This means it uses codes in the game to control your character which happens to be a legendary hero. You can control it's powers and movements with codes or programming languages that you've chosen. You will be able to fight other AI or a predetermined number of turns and the winner is the hero with the greatest amount of gold.
